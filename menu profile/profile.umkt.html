Profil UMKT


Muhammadiyah University of East Kalimantan (UMKT) is the result of merging STIKES Muhammadiyah STIE Muhammadiyah Samarinda and Samarinda with additions 10 new courses.UMKT has two main campus with an area of ​​15Ha. Campus 1 UMKT located in Complex Muhammadiyah Universities, Jl. Ir. H. Juanda No.. 15 and Campus 2 UMKT located at Jl. Pelita Complex Charm Mahakam. UMKT supported education in a very adequate learning facilities, ranging from classroom representative, a complete laboratory, and information technology-based library.
